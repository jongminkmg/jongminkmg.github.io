Research

The genome has numerous sites to initiate transcription, but a cell uses only a subset of those sites. We are interested in how chromatin structure and organization are modified to prevent spurious transcription. In particular, we will investigate how a burst of testis-specific transcription in spermatocytes is tightly regulated by Polycomb and CHD4-Zinc finger protein complexes. Our lab will explore the evolutionarily conserved repressive mechanisms safeguarding germ cell fate using flies and mice as model systems.
